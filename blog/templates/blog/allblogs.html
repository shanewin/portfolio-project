Executive Search & Recruiting

Success EdTech sales executive have to have the tenacity of a New York City real estate broker, along with the compassion of a social worker. The same high performing EdTech salespeople need to be tech savvy, organized, and feel the missionary zeal to provide super customer service whenever their schools need help. How do are you supposed to find a sales superstar with all these qualities - let alone many similar candidates to fulfill all of your sales responsibilities year-in and year-out? Partner with EdTech Sales Rep Consulting for all your recruiting, interviewing, and hiring needs.

EdTech Sales Rep Consulting will:

  • Employ a thorough approach designed towards identifying the ideal candidate for your role and your company
  • Evaluation of your organization's current sales procedures to determine what types of new hires could propel your company to future
  • Craft job descriptions that will attract attention from the highest caliber candidates on over-saturated job boards
  • Screen prospects with targeted questions to identify strengths and weaknesses specific to the EdTech field based on our in depth market knowledge
  • Define and work within time frame and budget for hiring

With extensive knowledge of the industry, the EdTech Sales Rep Consulting recruitment model takes an intensive approach to finding the best fit for your company.
