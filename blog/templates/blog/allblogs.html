Execute & Improve

Once you have invested time in EdTech market research, clearly defined your sales strategy, and hired the right sales superstars, your EdTech company will still have to recognize and maximize your best assets and resources, all the while adjusting your operations and processes to meet new challenges.

Every EdTech outfit needs an experienced sales manager to do this. However, there is no one-size-fits-all solution for every company. Depending on whether your EdTech company is in the start-up phase, growth phase, maturity phase, or any phase inbetween or beyond, it might be difficult to justify a full-time sales leader due to the overhead, time, and disruption.

For this reason, Ed Tech Sales Rep Consulting offers their clients sales management support on an ongoing or project basis in a direct, hands-on capacity. We can work in-house or remotely with your existing team on a temporary basis as the provisional sales manager, or even as a interim sales assoicate focused on "jump-starting" the prospecting channels and creating a valuable pipeline.
